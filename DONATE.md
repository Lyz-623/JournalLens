# Support JournalLens ♥ / 打赏支持

JournalLens is **free and open source**, and always will be. Development, testing and maintenance take real time — if this plugin saves you some, a small tip is hugely appreciated and keeps the updates coming!

JournalLens **永久免费开源**。开发、测试和维护都需要投入大量时间——如果这个插件帮你节省了时间,欢迎打赏支持,你的支持是持续更新的最大动力!
